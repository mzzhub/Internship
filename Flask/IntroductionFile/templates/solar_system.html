Interesting Facts

  • The Sun accounts for 99.8% of the mass in the solar system.
  • Jupiter is the largest planet, with a diameter of about 139,820 km.
  • Earth is the only known planet to support life.
  • Neptune has the strongest winds in the solar system, reaching up to 2,100 km/h.
